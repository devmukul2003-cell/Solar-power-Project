Smart Energy-Saving Tips

Personalized recommendations based on your energy usage patterns

🏠 Home Appliances

High Impact Save ₹500-800/month

Use LED Bulbs

Replace all incandescent bulbs with LED lights. They use 75% less energy and last 25 times longer.

Medium Impact Save ₹300-500/month

Optimize AC Usage

Set AC to 24-25°C and use timer mode. Each degree lower increases consumption by 6%.

Medium Impact Save ₹200-400/month

Unplug Devices

Unplug chargers and appliances when not in use. Phantom power accounts for 10% of electricity bills.

☀️ Solar Energy Optimization

High Impact Maximize savings

Peak Hour Usage

Run heavy appliances (washing machine, dishwasher) during peak solar hours (10 AM - 3 PM).

Medium Impact +15% efficiency

Panel Maintenance

Clean solar panels monthly. Dust and debris can reduce efficiency by 20-25%.

Smart Tip Better monitoring

Track Weather

Monitor weather forecasts to plan energy-intensive tasks on sunny days.

💡 Daily Habits

Easy Win Save ₹100-200/month

Natural Light

Open curtains during daytime. Natural lighting reduces electricity usage and improves mood.

Easy Win Save ₹150-250/month

Ceiling Fans First

Use ceiling fans before turning on AC. Fans use 98% less energy than air conditioners.

Medium Impact Save ₹200-300/month

Refrigerator Settings

Set fridge to 3-4°C and freezer to -18°C. Avoid keeping it too cold unnecessarily.
